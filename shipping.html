Order Processing

All orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed after 2pm may not be processed until the next business day. During peak seasons and promotional periods, processing times may be slightly longer.

Shipping Locations

We currently ship to all 50 United States, Canada, and select international destinations. For international orders, please allow 7-14 business days for delivery depending on the destination country.

Additional duties, taxes, and customs fees may apply for international shipments and are the responsibility of the customer.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number. You can also track your order anytime by:

  • Logging into your SoleStyle account
  • Using the tracking number in your confirmation email
  • Contacting our customer service team

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to:

  • Extreme weather conditions
  • Carrier service disruptions
  • Holiday peak periods
  • Incorrect shipping address provided

We will notify you of any significant delays affecting your order.

Missing or Damaged Packages

If your package appears to be lost or damaged during transit, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of the expected delivery date. We will work with the carrier to locate your package or process a replacement order.

Questions About Shipping?

If you have any questions regarding our shipping policy or the status of your order, please don't hesitate to contact us.
