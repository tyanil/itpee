Kids' Sizing Guide

How to Measure Your Child's Feet

  1. Have your child stand on a piece of paper with their heel against a wall.
  2. Mark the tip of their longest toe on the paper.
  3. Measure the distance from the wall to the mark in centimeters.
  4. Use our size chart to find the correct size.

We recommend measuring both feet and using the larger measurement. For growing kids, consider buying a half-size larger to allow room for growth.
