Product Details

The All-Terrain Hiking Boots are designed to provide maximum comfort and protection for hikers and outdoor enthusiasts. The boots feature a waterproof full-grain leather upper that keeps your feet dry in wet conditions, while the breathable Gore-Tex® membrane allows moisture to escape, keeping your feet comfortable during intense activities.

The Vibram® rubber outsole provides exceptional traction on various terrains, from muddy trails to rocky paths. The boots also feature a protective rubber toe cap that shields your feet from rocks and roots along the trail.

For comfort, the boots include an EVA midsole that absorbs shock and provides cushioning, and a removable OrthoLite® footbed that offers additional underfoot support. The padded collar and tongue protect your ankles from friction and pressure points.

Care Instructions

  • Clean dirt off with a soft brush
  • Apply leather conditioner regularly to maintain water resistance
  • Allow boots to dry naturally away from direct heat
  • Store in a cool, dry place
