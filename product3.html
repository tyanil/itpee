Leather Care Guide

To ensure the longevity of your Leather Oxford Dress Shoes, we recommend following these care instructions:

Regular Maintenance:

  • Clean your shoes after each wear with a soft brush to remove dust and dirt
  • Use cedar shoe trees to maintain shape and absorb moisture when not wearing
  • Allow at least 24 hours between wears to allow shoes to fully dry out

Deep Cleaning (Every 2-3 Months):

  1. Remove laces and clean them separately
  2. Wipe the shoes with a slightly damp cloth
  3. Apply a small amount of quality leather cleaner with a soft cloth
  4. Allow to dry naturally away from direct heat
  5. Apply leather conditioner to keep the leather supple
  6. Finish with a matching polish to maintain color and shine
  7. Buff with a horsehair brush for a perfect finish

Additional Tips:

  • Use a water repellent spray before first wear and reapply every few months
  • Rotate between different pairs of shoes to extend their life
  • Have your shoes resoled when the heel or sole shows significant wear
