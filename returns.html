Returns & Exchanges Policy

Our Guarantee

At SoleStyle, we want you to love your purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we're here to help with easy returns and exchanges.

Return Policy Highlights

30-Day Returns

Items in new, unworn condition can be returned within 30 days of delivery

Free Returns

Return shipping is free for all US orders (international returns may incur fees)

Easy Exchanges

Exchange for a different size or style with our simple exchange process

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return or exchange, your item must be:

  • In new, unworn condition
  • In the original packaging
  • Returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Accompanied by the original receipt or proof of purchase

Please note: Sale items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged.

How to Return or Exchange

  1. Initiate your return - Log in to your account and select the order containing the items you wish to return or exchange.
  2. Select items and reason - Choose the items you'd like to return or exchange and select a reason for your return.
  3. Print return label - Print the prepaid return label provided by our system.
  4. Package your return - Place the unworn items back in their original packaging, along with any tags attached.
  5. Ship your return - Drop off your package at any authorized shipping location.

Don't have access to a printer? Contact our customer service team to request a return label by email or mail.

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, our team will inspect the items. If approved:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original form of payment
  • Processing typically takes 3-5 business days
  • You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund is processed

Please allow up to 10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your financial institution.

Exchanges

Need a different size or color? We make exchanges easy:

  1. Follow the same process as returns, but select "Exchange" instead of "Return"
  2. Specify the new size, color, or style you'd like
  3. If the new item costs more than the original, you'll be prompted to pay the difference
  4. If the new item costs less, we'll refund the difference

Exchanges are subject to availability. If your requested item is out of stock, we'll contact you to discuss alternatives or process a refund.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange for a return shipping label and expedite your replacement or refund.

Please include photos of the damage or defect when contacting customer service to help expedite your claim.

International Returns

For international orders, return shipping fees are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective. Please contact our customer service team for specific instructions regarding international returns.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our return policy or need assistance with a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team.
