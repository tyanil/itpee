Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions

Ordering Questions

How do I place an order?

+

You can place an order through our website by browsing our collections, selecting your desired items, and adding them to your cart. Once you're ready to check out, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the shopping cart icon in the top right corner
  2. Review your items and click "Proceed to Checkout"
  3. Enter your shipping and billing information
  4. Select your preferred shipping method
  5. Enter payment details and complete your purchase

You'll receive an order confirmation email once your purchase is complete.

Can I modify or cancel my order after it's been placed?

+

We process orders quickly to ensure fast shipping. If you need to modify or cancel an order, please contact our customer service team immediately at orders@solestyle.com or call (800) 555-7654. We'll do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes once an order has been processed.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • SoleStyle Gift Cards

All transactions are secure and encrypted for your protection.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

+

Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for a small additional fee of $5 per item. During checkout, you'll have the option to select gift wrapping and include a personalized message. Your gift will be wrapped in premium paper with a SoleStyle ribbon and your message will be printed on a custom gift card.

How can I check the status of my order?

+

You can check your order status by:

  • Logging into your SoleStyle account and visiting the "Order History" section
  • Using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contacting our customer service team with your order number

Shipping Information

How long will it take to receive my order?

+

Domestic shipping typically takes 3-5 business days with our standard shipping option. Express shipping (1-2 business days) and Next Day delivery options are also available at checkout for an additional fee. Please note that processing time (typically 1-2 business days) is not included in these estimates.

For more detailed information, please visit our Shipping Policy page.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination country and local customs processing. Additional duties, taxes, and fees may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient.

How much does shipping cost?

+

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location, the size of your order, and your chosen shipping method. Standard domestic shipping is:

  • Free for orders over $75
  • $7.95 for orders under $75

Express shipping and international rates will be calculated at checkout. We occasionally offer free shipping promotions, so sign up for our newsletter to stay informed!

Can I ship to multiple addresses?

+

Currently, each order can only be shipped to a single address. If you need to ship items to multiple addresses, please place separate orders for each shipping destination.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn items in their original condition and packaging. Returns for refund or exchange are free for domestic orders. The original shipping cost is non-refundable, and international customers are responsible for return shipping fees.

For complete details, please visit our Returns & Exchanges page.

How do I return or exchange an item?

+

To initiate a return or exchange:

  1. Log into your SoleStyle account
  2. Go to "Order History" and select the order containing the item
  3. Click "Return or Exchange Items" and follow the instructions
  4. Print the prepaid return shipping label (domestic orders only)
  5. Package the item(s) in their original box and packaging
  6. Drop off the package at any authorized shipping location

You can also initiate a return by contacting our customer service team.

How long does it take to process a return?

+

Once we receive your return, it typically takes 3-5 business days to process. Refunds are issued to your original payment method and may take an additional 3-7 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your financial institution.

Can I return sale items?

+

Yes, most sale items are eligible for return within 30 days under our standard return policy. However, items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged unless they are defective. All clearance items are considered final sale.

Products & Sizing

How do I find the right shoe size?

+

We recommend using our interactive Size Guide to find your perfect fit. The guide includes:

  • Detailed measurement instructions
  • Conversion charts for international sizing
  • Brand-specific fit recommendations
  • Width guide for narrow, regular, and wide feet

If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for a more comfortable fit.

Are your products authentic?

+

Absolutely! SoleStyle is an authorized retailer for all brands we carry. We source our products directly from manufacturers and authorized distributors, ensuring that every pair of shoes we sell is 100% authentic. We have a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit products.

How do I care for my shoes?

+

Proper care extends the life of your footwear. General care tips include:

  • Clean shoes regularly with appropriate cleaners for the material
  • Allow shoes to dry naturally away from direct heat
  • Use shoe trees to maintain shape when not wearing
  • Apply protective sprays for leather, suede, and canvas shoes
  • Rotate your footwear to allow shoes to rest between wears

For material-specific care guides, visit our Shoe Care page.

Do you offer wide or narrow width shoes?

+

Yes, many of our styles are available in multiple width options. Look for the width indicator next to the size selection on product pages:

  • N: Narrow width
  • M: Medium/Standard width
  • W: Wide width
  • XW: Extra Wide width

You can also filter our collection by width when browsing.

My Account Questions

How do I create an account?

+

Creating a SoleStyle account is easy and free. Click on "My Account" in the top navigation, then select "Create Account." You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. You can also sign up during checkout.

Benefits of having an account include:

  • Faster checkout process
  • Order tracking and history
  • Ability to create wishlists
  • Access to exclusive offers and early sales
  • Rewards program enrollment

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

+

To reset your password:

  1. Click on "My Account" in the top navigation
  2. Select "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the link to create a new password

The reset link expires after 24 hours for security purposes.

How do I update my account information?

+

You can update your account information by logging into your account and visiting the "Account Settings" section. Here you can modify:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Password
  • Saved addresses
  • Payment methods
  • Communication preferences

What is the SoleStyle Rewards Program?

+

SoleStyle Rewards is our loyalty program that lets you earn points with every purchase. Points can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

  • Earn 1 point for every $1 spent
  • 100 points = $5 off your next purchase
  • Birthday bonus: Double points during your birthday month
  • Exclusive member-only promotions and early access to sales
  • Free shipping on all orders (no minimum) for members who reach Elite status

Enrollment is automatic when you create an account. Visit our Rewards Program page for complete details.
